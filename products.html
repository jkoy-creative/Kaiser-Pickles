OUR PRODUCTS

Kaiser Pickles sources over 95% of our pickles and peppers from local farms in the Ohio, Indiana, and Michigan area. These raw materials are distributed between our three manufacturing facilities (two locations in Cincinnati, OH and one location in Aurora, IN), these facilities are all SQF Level 3 certified. Our long list of quality assurance programs, ensure we consistently produce the safest and highest quality products that our customers have come to expect and love.
